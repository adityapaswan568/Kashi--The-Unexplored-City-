# Kashi: The Unexplored City

A beautiful, SEO-optimized static website exploring the lesser-known spiritual dimensions and hidden cultural treasures of Varanasi (Kashi).

## About the Project

**Kashi: The Unexplored City** is a BCA-level educational project that shines a light on the unexplored spiritual and tourist dimensions of Varanasi. While the city is globally recognized for the Kashi Vishwanath Temple and Dashashwamedh Ghat, this project focuses on discovering the deeper spiritual fabric through lesser-known temples, hidden ghats, sacred wells, and living ritual traditions that define authentic Kashi.

### Project Goals
- Highlight lesser-known spiritual sites in Varanasi
- Provide comprehensive information about hidden temples and sacred locations
- Present Kashi's rich history and cultural significance
- Create an engaging and informative digital experience for spiritual tourists

## Technology Stack

- **HTML5** - Semantic and SEO-friendly markup
- **CSS3** - Responsive design and styling
- **Vanilla JavaScript** - Interactive features and functionality
- **Static Website** - No framework, no backend, no database

4. **Explore** the timeline by visiting `timeline.html`

## Content Highlights

### Historical Context
- Mentioned in ancient texts (Rigveda, Skanda Purana, Mahabharata)
- Known as "City of Light" (Kashi = "to shine")
- Believed to be established by Lord Shiva
- Major center for multiple spiritual traditions

### Key Locations
- **Sankata Devi Temple** - Shakti temple for removal of distress
- **Kal Bhairav Temple** - Known for intense spiritual atmosphere
- **Gyanvapi Well** - Sacred well with mythological significance
- **Lesser-known Ghats** - Hidden bathing points along the Ganga
